Meet the Chef

Veronique is a native of Paris, France.

She was raised into the French tradition where bread and pastries were passed down through the generations. All of the food made fresh, daily, with love!

Her passion for baking was ignited in 2015. She wanted to recreate the french pastries from her childhood that were missing around her. She enrolled in a local college with an extensive baking program where a French chef taught the authentic European Tradition.

Her true passion for baking and bringing a taste of Europe was coming true!



Our Mission

Our mission is to create an Incredible authentic culinary experience.
I want each bite of buttery flaky taste of heaven to melt in your mouth!

I use only the Freshest Ingredients like European butter, Almond ,fresh Fruits.

I will strive to provide my customers the best quality baked items with no additives of chemicals.
